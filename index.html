Research Interests

I am an astrophysicist focused on understanding the evolution of the star-formation rate density (SFRD) and the nature of galaxies in the early Universe. My work utilizes deep multi-wavelength observations, from the ultraviolet to the far-infrared and submillimeter regimes.

  • The Dusty Universe: Using data from ALMA and SCUBA-2, I study submillimeter galaxies (SMGs) to reveal star formation obscured by dust.
  • Cosmic Dawn: Analysis of high-redshift luminosity functions ($z > 3$) to constrain cosmological models.
